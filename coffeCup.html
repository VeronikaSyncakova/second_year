Star bean

About

This was voluntary project in a team of 5 for a competition Called Games Fleadh that is sponsored by companies such as EA. Our team consisted of 3 programmers, one artist and one sound designer. Everything in the game was made by us. As this was work outside of our school time and we were working on it remotely, we had to learn how to properly function in a team. We created this game in 3 weeks including the planning phase. Communication was key as we had to adjust the workflow during the stressful times, long nights and daily demos of the progress. Luckly the hard work paid off as it won 2 awards at the competition. It was our first released game and now you can download it from itch.io and play it on your machine.

It is a coffee shop simulation. You are playing as a space barista and you want to keep the customers happy, earn money and develop you skills through different achievements.

Some things we were proud of include radial menus with easing functions, pre-caching of assets for faster execution, finite state machine, singleton pattern.

Technology

C++, SFML library, text file

Assets
